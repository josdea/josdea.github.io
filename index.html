About Me

Instructional technology specialist at Pellissippi State Community College. I specialize in Learning Management Systems such as: Brightspace D2L, BlackBoard, and Canvas. I also work with accessible content for the web, virtual classrooms with Adobe Connect, and other educational technologies.

The more I learn the more my interests change. I've been learning more and more about Oracle SQL databses, javascript, and javascript frameworks like VueJS.
